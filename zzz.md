### **Treatment Recommendation for Lung Adenocarcinoma with EGFR (c.2739_1251delins, p.L747_T751delins), MED12, TP53, CDKN2A, and SMAD4 Mutations**  

#### **Primary Treatment**  
**First-line therapy**: **Osimertinib (80 mg once daily)** is the **preferred first-line treatment** for this patient with advanced/metastatic lung adenocarcinoma harboring an **EGFR exon 19 deletion-like mutation (p.L747_T751delins)**. Osimertinib is a **third-generation EGFR TKI** with superior CNS penetration and efficacy compared to earlier-generation TKIs (Reference: **10.6004/jnccn.2204.0023**).  

#### **Rationale for Osimertinib Over Other EGFR TKIs**  
- The patient’s **EGFR p.L747_T751delins** is a **non-classical exon 19 deletion variant**, but osimertinib has demonstrated efficacy in atypical EGFR mutations (e.g., L861Q, S768I, G719X) (Reference: **10.6004/jnccn.2204.0023**).  
- **Co-occurring TP53 and CDKN2A loss** suggest a more aggressive tumor biology, where osimertinib’s broader activity may be beneficial.  
- **MED12 and SMAD4 mutations** are not directly targetable but may influence resistance mechanisms, necessitating close monitoring.  

#### **Subsequent Therapy Considerations**  
If progression occurs on osimertinib:  
1. **Re-biopsy and liquid biopsy (ctDNA) for resistance mechanisms**:  
   - If **T790M or C797S mutations** emerge, consider **amivantamab-vmjw + chemotherapy (carboplatin/pemetrexed)** (Reference: **10.6004/jnccn.2204.0023**, **PMC10425724**).  
   - If **histologic transformation to SCLC** is detected, switch to **platinum-etoposide chemotherapy** (Reference: **PMC11369616**).  
2. **If no actionable resistance mutation is found**:  
   - **Chemotherapy (pemetrexed + carboplatin/cisplatin) ± bevacizumab** (for non-squamous histology).  

#### **Adjunct Therapies & Supportive Care**  
- **Prophylactic cranial irradiation (PCI)**: Not routinely recommended unless SCLC transformation occurs.  
- **Bone-modifying agents (e.g., zoledronic acid)**: If bone metastases are present.  
- **Palliative care referral**: For symptom management (e.g., pain, dyspnea).  

#### **Additional Considerations**  
- **Baseline brain MRI**: Due to osimertinib’s CNS efficacy but risk of progression in TP53/CDKN2A-altered tumors.  
- **Serial ctDNA monitoring**: To detect resistance mutations early (e.g., T790M, C797S, MET amplification).  
- **Avoid PD-1/PD-L1 inhibitors**: Poor efficacy in EGFR-mutant NSCLC (Reference: **10.6004/jnccn.2204.0023**).  

### **Final Recommendation**  
**"Recommendation: First-line therapy with osimertinib (80 mg once daily) for advanced lung adenocarcinoma with EGFR p.L747_T751delins mutation. Upon progression, perform re-biopsy/ctDNA to assess for resistance mechanisms (e.g., T790M, C797S, SCLC transformation) and consider amivantamab-vmjw + chemotherapy or platinum-etoposide if SCLC transformation occurs. Additional considerations: Baseline brain MRI, serial ctDNA monitoring, and palliative care for symptom management."**
